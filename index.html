A little About Me

I am currently working in Bangladesh Police as Additional Superintendent of Police. I was born in 1985 in Dhowra village under Shailkupa police station in Jhenaidah district of Bangladesh. I passed the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations from my village and obtained my Bachelor's degree in English from Dhaka University. First I joined Rupali Bank Limited as Senior Officer, then joined ACC as Assistant Director and then joined 29th BCS as Assistant Commissioner and Executive Magistrate. Later joined 30th BCS as Assistant Superintendent of Police (ASP).
